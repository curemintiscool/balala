As a human, Azure has long silver curly hair and big ice blue eyes, slightly slanted bangs, white skin, and delicate facial features. She wears a blue headband, a blue coat, a dark blue dress, and a pair of blue sandals. She wears a cyan shell-shaped ring with a bracelet on her left hand. As a fairy, she has long straight blue hair with yellow shades and long unilateral bangs. Her eyes turn bright blue and gain yellow stars. She wears a white headband on the head (wings on both sides of the headband), a blue dress with a translucent sky blue layer, and white over-the-knee boots with some cyan and yellow patterns. She has stingray wings on her back. Her headband remains the same in her princess form, but she gains a fan-shaped crown in the middle. She wears a teal cape with fluffy white trimming attached to a golden button that holds the cape. There is also a long blue rhombus that attaches to the top and the cape. She loses her sleeves, and her top slightly changes and has a blue gem in the middle. Her gloves become longer and become white. The top of her gloves has a dark blue crown-shaped border. The belt that holds her skirt is yellow with a blue diamond in the middle. Her long dress has a short dark blue layer with a yellow border. The rest of her skirt is white.

Azure's last name, 娜 (nà), means "elegant". Her Chinese first name, 希雅, (xī yǎ), means "hopeful and stylish", with 希 (xī), meaning "hope", and 雅 (yǎ), meaning stylish. Azure means "Sky Blue" in Spanish.
